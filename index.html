ABOUT THE GAME

I love The Legend of Zelda

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an open world, action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo in 2017 for the Nintendo Switch consoles. In this game, the player controls Link, who awakens from a hundred-year slumber to defeat Calamity Ganon and save the great kingdom of Hyrule. The world packed with endless opportunities, possibilities, and exploration. This game is very dear to me. Without a doubt, it is one of the greatest games I have ever played and ever will play.

Why Breath of the Wild?


There is no doubt that the art in this game is just as breathtaking as the previous installments. The art in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is by far my favorite amongst the franchise. Whomever you ask may also share this view, with many players still in awe from the beautiful scenery three and a half years after its initial release. This fact did not go unnoticed either, as the game has won countless rewards for its beauty and detail, but all this detail did not take a day to create. The creators of the game struggled for a long time to achieve the perfect style for their game. There were many drafts and concepts thrown away when they were decided not to be the right choice for the game. We can imagine the endless amount of hours the artists put into this project. A lot of this work has unfortunately never been able to see the light of day, but thanks to the determination of the creators, we have a beautiful final product. The concepts for Zelda alone filled more than three pages, and although they were never used, it's amazing to be able to look back on the possibilities and directions my favorite game could have taken.
